About Mohan Air

Who are we?

We are a coaching, mentoring and consulting firm dedicated to nurturing curiosity, confidence and a life-long passion in aspiring aviators and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To empower, build, inspire and guide aspiring aviators to achieve their dreams and more from a young age. We purpose to provide high-quality services that ensure complete client satisfaction while creating and cultivating long-term relationships with clients. We believe that anyone with a passion for flight deserves expert mentorship and resources to achieve their dreams.

Our Vision

To be the premier community for aviation enthusiasts, fostering skill, knowledge, and camaraderie in the pursuit of flight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Please reach us at mohanair.aviationcoaching@gmail.com if you cannot find an answer to your question.

What is Mohan Air Club all about?

Mohan Air Aviation Club is a fun, engaging program that introduces kids and teens to the world of aviation through hands-on learning, mentorship, STEM-based activities, and career exploration.

Do members get to fly real planes?

Yes! Members will have the chance to experience discovery flights with certified instructors. All members get simulator training and hands-on aviation experiences.

Who are the mentors and instructors?

Our mentors include real pilots, aviation engineers, air traffic controllers, flight attendants and aviation enthusiasts. All passionate about guiding the next generation.

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer programs for various age groups starting from 5 years old up to adults. Our Junior Aviators program is designed for ages 5-8, while we have specialized programs for teens and adults as well.

What safety measures are in place?

Safety is our top priority. All our flight activities are conducted with certified instructors and maintained aircraft. We follow strict safety protocols for all our programs and maintain a safe learning environment.
